Dear David,

I have completed the evaluation of the four tenders received for the Riverside Commercial Fitout project. This report summarises my findings and provides a clear recommendation for your consideration.

Recommended contractor: Apex Integrated Services at $1,890,000 i. Apex submitted the most competitive fully-conforming tender, the shortest programme at 16 weeks i, and a complete scope with no material exclusions. Of the four tenders received, two were conforming (Apex and Meridian Build Group) and one was non-conforming (Pacific Peak Builders).

While Pacific Peak submitted the lowest headline price at $1,870,000, their tender excluded HVAC, plumbing, fire services, and provisional sums. When these items are priced in at market rates, Pacific Peak's true comparable cost exceeds $2.5M i — making them the most expensive option and unsuitable for award.

Meridian Build Group is my second preference at $1,940,000 i, subject to resolution of their HVAC exclusion prior to contract execution. CoreTrade Solutions, while fully conforming, submitted a price $295,000 above the recommendation with a 20-week programme and cannot be recommended on value grounds.

The interactive report below provides full pricing breakdowns, scope inclusions, programme comparisons, and risk analysis for each contractor. I recommend issuing a Letter of Intent to Apex Integrated Services at your earliest convenience to secure their programme commitment.

Please don't hesitate to contact me to discuss.
